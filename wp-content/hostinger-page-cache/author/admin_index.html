Little About Life

  1. Be a mental engineer and use tried and proven techniques in building a grander and greater life.  
  2. Your desire is your prayer. Picture the fulfillment of your desire now and feel its reality, and you will experience the joy of answered prayer.  
  3. You can build radiant health,  success and happiness by the thoughts you think in the hidden studio of your mind.  
  4. Experiment scientifically until you personally prove that there is always a direct response from the infinite intelligence of your sub conscious mind to your conscious thinking.  
  5. Avoid all effort or mental coercion in prayer. Get into a sleepy, drowsy state and lull yourself to sleep feeling and knowing that your prayer is answered.  
  6. Remember that thankful heart is always close to the riches of the universe.  
  7. Generate waves of harmony, health and peace by thinking of love and glory of god. 
  8. Patience, kindness, love, goodwill, joy, happiness, wisdom and understanding are the qualities never grow old. Cultivate them and express them and remain young in body and mind.  
  9. God is life, and that is your life now. Life is self renewing, eternal and indestructible and is the reality of all humans. You live forever, your life is God’s life.  
  10. You are child of infinite life that knows no end. You are an heir to eternity.  
  11. You cannot see your mind but you know you have a mind. You cannot see spirit, but you know that the spirit of game, the spirit of artist, the spirit of musician and the spirit of speaker is real. Likewise the spirit of goodness, truth, and beauty moving in your mind and heart are real. You cannot see life, but you know you are alive.  
  12. You are as young you think you are.  You are as strong you think you are.  You are as useful as you think you are. You are as young as your thoughts.  
  13. Become a producer and not a prisoner of society. Don’t hide your light under a bushel.  
  14. The secret of youth is love, joy, inner price and laughter. In God there is fullness of joy. In God there is no darkness at all.  
  15. When your mind is relaxed and you accept an idea, your subconscious goes to work to execute the idea .
  16. You avoid conflict between your subconscious and conscious in the sleepy state. Imagine the full fillment of your desire over and over again prior to sleep. Sleep in peace and wake in joy.
